# **App Name**: DivineConnect

## Core Features:

- News Feed: Display current spiritual news and updates in a scrollable feed.
- Satsang Video Gallery: Allow users to view a curated selection of Satsang videos.
- Photo Gallery: Showcase spiritual photos.
- Bookstore: List and sell spiritual books, linking to external stores or providing purchase options.
- AI-Powered Content Suggestions: Recommend relevant news, videos, and books to users based on their viewing history. An AI tool suggests potentially relevant material.
- Join Form: A simple form to allow users to sign up.
- FAQ Section: Provide answers to frequently asked questions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Indigo (#3F51B5) to evoke spirituality and tranquility.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F0F0) for a clean, calming backdrop.
- Accent color: Gold (#FFD700) to highlight important elements and call to mind enlightenment.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya', a humanist serif to bring an elegant and intellectual feel.
- Use simple, elegant icons relevant to spirituality and enlightenment.
- Clean, uncluttered layout with plenty of white space to promote a sense of calm.
- Subtle animations for transitions and loading to enhance user experience without being distracting.
